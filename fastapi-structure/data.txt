The solar system consists of the Sun and eight planets. The planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. 
Jupiter is the largest planet, with a diameter of about 139,820 kilometers. Mercury is the smallest, with a diameter of about 4,880 kilometers. 
The Sun is a star, not a planet, and it provides the energy that sustains life on Earth. Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance.
